Marketing
In a world where identity is everything, it is more critical than ever for brands to know how package themselves for their audience. In marketing, we learn how to effectively analyze, predict, and shape consumer reactions in specific industry landscapes. In addition, we will develop an important tool box of professional skills to assist in marketing ourselves in a business environment.
